Biotech in 2025: A Map of Money and Roles


The biotech industry continues to shift as new technologies emerge, funding tightens, and job roles evolve.


This data story explores how salaries and layoffs have changed between 2022 and 2025, highlighting the increasing levels of instability and competition for professionals working in this field.

1. Salary Trends in the Biotech Industry


Biotech salaries vary widely, with some roles not seeing stable increases between 2022-2025.

Data obtained from the r/biotech Salary Survey.

Average salaries for the 10 most common biotech roles

Data shown includes all salaries from any location, averaged for all respodents reporting a given role.
Director salaries increase steadily, while lab-based roles such as Research Associate and Scientist see limited growth or even a decline in compensation over time.
